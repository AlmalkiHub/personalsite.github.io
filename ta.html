Text Analytics (TA) Coursework

Project Overview

In this coursework, I developed a text analytics system and demonstrated what I have learned in this unit, applied my knowledge in a practical situation, and critically analysed a text analytics system.



The project consists of the following tasks:

  • - Extracting entities from scientific abstracts.
  • - Extracting semantic relations.
  • - A critical review of the scientific paper: Ribeiro, Marco Tulio, et al. "Beyond Accuracy: Behavioral Testing of NLP Models with CheckList." ACL 2020.
